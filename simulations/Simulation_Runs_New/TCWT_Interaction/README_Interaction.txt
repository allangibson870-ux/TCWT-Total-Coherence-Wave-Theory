# TCWT Phase-Bleed Interaction (Version 2026.9)
## Files Included:
- tcwt_interaction.py: Models the linear superposition of two Hum phase-knots.
- tcwt_interaction_map.png: 2D visualization of the resulting gravitational wells.
- README_Interaction.txt: Metadata for the interaction simulation.

## Technical Insight:
In TCWT, gravitational attraction is the result of 'constructive interference' 
between phase gradients. The 'Phase-Bleed' (acceleration field) is strongest 
in the region between the knots, providing the mechanism for attraction 
without requiring a separate force carrier.
