# TCWT Force Scaling Analysis (Version 2026.9)
## Files Included:
- tcwt_force_curve.py: Compares phase-gradient decay to Newtonian gravity.
- tcwt_force_scaling.png: Log-Log plot showing the regime transition.

## Analysis:
In the 'Near Field' (left of plot), the acceleration follows the knot's internal 
geometry. In the 'Far Field' (right), the theory predicts a deviation from 
Newtonian 1/r^2 toward the MOND regime as the gradient approaches a0.
