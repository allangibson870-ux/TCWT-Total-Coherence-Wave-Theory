# TCWT Stress Test: The Thermal Floor

## The Hypothesis
In **TCWT**, heat is modeled as "Stochastic Informational Pressure." Every phonon (vibration) acts as a microscopic observer that increases the local **$\Omega*.

## The Result
This simulation identifies a **Thermal Transition Point** (The "Boiling Point of Coherence"). 
- At low temperatures, the **Background Hum** dominates, and the **Knot** is stable.
- Around **150K**, the thermal noise exceeds the **$\kappa* threshold, leading to a rapid collapse into the classical state.

## Why it matters
This explains why quantum computers require dilution refrigerators. It isn't just about "noise"—it's about preventing the environment from "computing" the qubits through thermal interaction.
