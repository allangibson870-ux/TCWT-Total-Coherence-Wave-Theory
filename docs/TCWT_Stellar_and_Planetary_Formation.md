$$k_* \propto \sqrt{\frac{M_*}{M_\odot}}$$

$$\lambda_* = \frac{2\pi}{k_*}$$

$$\lambda_* \propto \sqrt{M_*}$$

$$r_n \propto \sqrt{M_*}$$

- low‑mass stars → **compressed** planetary systems
